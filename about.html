Generva

Generva by DartDiagnose is an app for medical professionals, patients, and patient's families to help diagnose rare genetic and neurometabolic diseases. Generva was built by neurologist Dr. James Filiano, neuroscientist Dr. David Graber, and the DALI Lab at Dartmouth College. This app is an online selection quiz of clinical signs and symptoms. It provides relative probabilities of diagnoses, and refers the user to lists of “differential diagnosis” found in the book "Simplified Diagnosis of Neurometabolic Disease" by Dr. Filiano.
